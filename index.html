Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 26, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Saviolux ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application ("Saviolux", "the app").

Information We Collect

1. Personal Data

  • Emergency Contact Number: If you choose to use the emergency SMS feature, you may enter and save a phone number. This number is stored locally on your device and is only used to send SOS messages when you request it.

2. Usage Data

  • We do not collect or transmit any personal or usage data to our servers or any third parties.
  • The app does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

3. Device Permissions

To provide its core features, Saviolux requests the following permissions:

  • Camera: To use the flashlight for Morse code signaling.
  • Microphone: To enable the "Listen" feature (if available).
  • Location: To include your location in SOS messages (only when you use the emergency SMS feature).
  • SMS: To send SOS messages to your chosen contact.
  • Vibration: For haptic feedback.
  • Foreground Service: To keep the app running for certain features (e.g., Morse code signaling).

How We Use Your Information

  • Emergency Contact: The phone number you provide is used only to send SOS messages at your request.
  • Location: Your location is included in SOS messages only when you use the emergency SMS feature.
  • No Data Sharing: We do not share your information with any third parties.
  • No Account Required: You do not need to create an account to use Saviolux.

Data Storage

  • All data (such as your emergency contact) is stored locally on your device.
  • We do not store or transmit your data to any external servers.

Children's Privacy

Saviolux does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete such information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app or on our website. Please review this policy periodically for updates.
